Goals

The Paris Agreement aims to promote the global climate change response by increasing the ability of all to adverse effects of climate changes and fosters climate resilience.the goal is:

  • to enhance adaptivity and relience
  • To reduce vulnerability, in order to achieve sustainable development;
  • to ensure adequate adaptive response in combarting of global warming

Planning and implementation

The agreement requires all parties as appropriate to engage in adaptation planning and implemantation through;

  • National adaptation plans
  • vulnerable assessment
  • Monitoring and evaluation
  • Economic diversification

Trasparency

The Agreement will improve the umderstanding of climate change and the transparency of actons by establishing a new framework that should provide information related to climate change impacts and adaptation
