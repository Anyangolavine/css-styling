The red cross is humanitarian organisation that aims at promoting sustainability, health well being and resilience of community. it provides relief services during emergencies ,crisis and pandemic. it implements programme that aim to lessen or transfer the adverse effects of hazards through preparedness,migration and responses

The National Environment Management Authority (NEMA) is anational entity, which was establish in 2002 with the objectives of supervising and coordinating environmental activities and serving as the main national body to implement environmental policies in all sectors within the country ,It is mandated to write and enforce laws and regulation for environmental,to educate the public about the nations environmental issues and to control and to prevent pollution, as well as conserve natural resources

The Food and Organisation (FAO) is an international organisation that leads international effort and improve nurition and food security . ITS motto is"let there be bread". it was founded on 16th october aim of cubbing hunger and promoting good health through nurition . It plays amajor role in climate change involved in coming up and implements ways of dealing with climate change and its adverse effects.

UNEP mission is to provide leadership and encourages partnership in caring for the environment by inspiring, informing and enabling nation and people to improve the quality of life without compromising that of future generation it works on delivery transformational change for people and nature bydrilling down on the root cause of the three planetary crises of climate change nature and biodiversity loss,pollution and waste
